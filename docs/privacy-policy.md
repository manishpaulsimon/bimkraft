# BIMKraft Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 26, 2025**

## Introduction

BIMKraft ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software and services.

This policy complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address (required for license delivery)
- Name (optional, for personalized communication)
- Company name (optional)
- Billing address (for VAT compliance)

**Payment Information:**
- Processed by Stripe (our payment processor)
- We do NOT store credit card numbers or payment details
- We receive transaction confirmations and billing history from Stripe

**Support Communications:**
- Emails, attachments, and screenshots you send to support
- Chat transcripts (if using live chat)
- Feedback and survey responses

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data

**License Validation Data:**
- License key
- Activation date and time
- Machine fingerprint (hardware ID - anonymized)
- Revit version installed
- Operating system version
- Plugin version

**Usage Analytics (Anonymous):**
- Feature usage statistics (which tools you use, how often)
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics (load times, response times)
- Session duration

**Website Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referral source
- Cookies and similar technologies

### 1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

- Your Revit project files or model data
- Element data or parameters from your projects
- Personal information within your Revit models
- Passwords or credentials for other services
- Location data (beyond IP-based country detection)

### 5.1 Where We Store Data

**Primary Storage:**

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)

- Secure database access controls
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Two-factor authentication for admin access

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Limited employee access (need-to-know basis)
- Confidentiality agreements with staff and contractors

**Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.**

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a European resident, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
- Response time: Within 30 days

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Update your email, name, or billing information
- Via account dashboard or email request

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Exceptions: Legal obligations, ongoing contracts, legitimate interests
- Does not apply to anonymized analytics data

### 6.4 Right to Restriction
- Limit how we use your data while we verify a dispute
- During legal proceedings
- Request via email with justification

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability
- Receive your data in JSON or CSV format
- Transfer data to another service provider
- Available for data you provided to us

### 6.6 Right to Object
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt out of marketing communications (always honored)
- We will stop processing unless we have compelling legal grounds

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw consent for marketing or optional processing anytime
- Does not affect lawfulness of prior processing
- Use unsubscribe links or contact privacy@bimkraft.de

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
- File a complaint with your national data protection authority
