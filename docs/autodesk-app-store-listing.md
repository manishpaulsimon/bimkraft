## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Supercharge Your Revit Workflow with BIMKraft**

BIM Kraft Tools is a comprehensive suite of 6 professional-grade tools designed to dramatically increase productivity in Autodesk Revit. Whether you're managing complex projects, collaborating with large teams, or performing detailed quality control, BIMKraft streamlines your workflow and saves hours every week.

**üéØ What You Get**

**Parameter Pro**
Advanced parameter management for power users. Batch edit parameters across thousands of elements, create and manage shared parameters, filter and search with precision, and save parameter configurations as reusable presets. Perfect for maintaining consistency across projects and project templates.

**Parameter Transfer Pro**
Intelligently transfer parameter values between different element types. Create flexible mappings (source ‚Üí target), process entire categories or selections, and save transfer configurations for recurring workflows. Ideal for copying design data, transferring specifications, and maintaining parameter consistency.

**Workset Manager**
Rule-based workset assignment for efficient collaboration. Apply 6 built-in presets for common scenarios (architectural, structural, MEP) or create custom rules based on category, class, parameter values, type names, or family names. Automatically creates worksets and processes entire projects in seconds.

**Warnings Browser Pro**
Advanced warning analysis and resolution tracking. Filter by severity, category, or keywords. Group similar warnings for batch resolution. Export to Excel/CSV for team tracking. Zoom directly to problematic elements. Essential for maintaining model health and passing quality checks.

**Line Length Calculator**
Calculate connected line lengths with precision. Auto-groups lines by style and connectivity. Assign colors for visualization. Apply color overrides to lines in views. Export detailed reports to Excel/CSV. Create Revit groups from line groups. Perfect for detail line takeoffs and construction quantity estimates.

**Family Renamer**
Batch rename families with powerful operations. Add prefixes or suffixes, find and replace text, apply sequential numbering, and remove unwanted characters. Preview changes before applying. Filter by category or search. Maintain naming standards across projects effortlessly.

## Installation Instructions

1. Download the MSI installer from bimkraft.de/download
2. Close Autodesk Revit if running
3. Run the installer (BIMKraft_0.1.0_2026.msi)
4. Follow on-screen instructions
5. Accept the license agreement
6. Choose installation location (default recommended)
7. Click "Install"
8. Launch Revit
9. Look for the "BIMKraft" ribbon tab
10. Click any tool to start your 30-day trial

**Installation Location**:
- Plugin: `C:\ProgramData\Autodesk\ApplicationPlugins\BIMKraft.bundle\`
- Add-in manifest: `%AppData%\Autodesk\Revit\Addins\[Version]\BIMKraft.addin`

---

## Uninstallation Instructions

1. Close Autodesk Revit
2. Open Windows Settings ‚Üí Apps
3. Find "BIM Kraft Tools" in the list
4. Click "Uninstall"
5. Follow on-screen prompts
6. Restart Revit

**Manual Uninstallation** (if needed):
1. Delete: `C:\ProgramData\Autodesk\ApplicationPlugins\BIMKraft.bundle\`
2. Delete: `%AppData%\Autodesk\Revit\Addins\[Version]\BIMKraft.addin`
3. Delete settings: `%AppData%\BIMKraft\`

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does BIMKraft work with Revit LT?**
A: No, BIMKraft requires full Autodesk Revit (2023-2026). Revit LT's API limitations prevent add-in functionality.

**Q: Can I use BIMKraft on multiple computers?**
A: Yes, one license allows installation on up to 3 computers. Activate your license key on each machine.

**Q: Is the trial fully functional?**
A: Yes! The 30-day trial includes complete access to all 6 tools with no limitations. No credit card required.

**Q: What happens after the trial expires?**
A: After 30 days, you'll need to purchase a license to continue using BIMKraft. Your trial data and presets are preserved.

**Q: Do you offer team or enterprise licenses?**
A: Yes! Contact sales@bimkraft.de for volume discounts on 5+ licenses and custom enterprise solutions.

**Q: Is VAT included in the price?**
A: Prices shown exclude VAT. VAT will be added at checkout based on your location (EU customers only).

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period with no early termination fees.

**Q: Does BIMKraft collect my project data?**
A: No. BIMKraft only collects anonymous usage statistics (which features you use) to improve the software. Your Revit models and project data remain completely private.

**Q: How do I get support?**
A: Email support@bimkraft.de or visit bimkraft.de/documentation. We typically respond within 1-2 business days.

**Q: Will BIMKraft work with future Revit versions?**
A: We update BIMKraft for new Revit releases within 30-60 days of launch. Active subscribers receive free updates.
